Thanks!

Thank you for taking the time to share your rates. Your participation helps to build the world's first comprehensive, public database of water rate information, and will serve as a resource for generations to come. Together we can advance our understanding of water pricing to help ensure a reliable water supply for all.


As a token of our appreciation, you will be emailed with login credentials to access the CaDC Statewide Efficiency Explorer. The tool is a first assessment of the longterm efficiency framework under development in California and serves a planning tool to help California water agencies understand where their water efficiency relative to these efficiency benchmarks. Stay tuned!
